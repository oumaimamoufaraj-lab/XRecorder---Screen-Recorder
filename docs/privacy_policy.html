Privacy Policy

XRecorder - Screen Recorder

Last updated: June 1, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to XRecorder - Screen Recorder (“XRecorder”, “we”, “our”, or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application (the “App”) on iOS and Android devices.

Summary: XRecorder is designed to record your screen and help you manage recordings on your device. Your recordings stay on your device unless you choose to share them. We do not operate a user account system and we do not upload your screen recordings to our servers.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

Information We Collect

1. Screen recordings and media on your device

When you record your screen, the video is saved to your device’s Photos library (or local storage, depending on your device and permissions). XRecorder may read recordings from your Photos library so you can view, play, share, or delete them inside the App.

We do not receive or store your recordings on our servers.

2. Microphone audio (optional)

If you enable the microphone during a screen broadcast or recording session, audio may be included in your recording. Microphone access is used only when you turn it on. We do not access your microphone in the background without your action.

3. App preferences stored on your device

The App may store settings locally on your device, such as:

  • Onboarding completion status
  • Appearance / theme preference (Light, Dark, or System)
  • Other in-app settings needed for the App to function

This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

4. Advertising and analytics data

The App displays advertisements through third-party ad partners, including Google AdMob. These partners may collect or receive certain information to deliver and measure ads, such as:

  • Device identifiers (for example, advertising ID)
  • IP address (which may be used to infer general location)
  • App usage related to ad delivery
  • Consent choices where required by law

In regions where required (such as the European Economic Area and United Kingdom), we use Google’s User Messaging Platform (UMP) to collect and manage your advertising consent preferences. You can change ad privacy choices in the App where available (Settings → Ad privacy choices).

For more information about how Google uses data, visit: Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

5. Remote configuration

The App may download configuration data needed to operate advertising features (for example, ad unit settings). This does not include your personal screen recordings.

How We Use Information

We use information only to:

  • Provide screen recording and video management features
  • Save, display, and organize your recordings on your device
  • Enable optional sharing through your device’s share sheet
  • Display and measure advertisements
  • Respect your consent and privacy choices where required by law
  • Improve App stability and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

Permissions We Request

  • Photos / Media library: to save screen recordings and show them in the Videos tab
  • Microphone: only when you enable audio during recording
  • Screen recording / Broadcast: to capture your screen using system recording APIs

You can manage or revoke permissions in your device Settings at any time. Some App features may not work without the required permissions.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your screen recordings.

Information may be shared only in these cases:

  • When you choose to share: if you use Share, your recording is shared through your device’s system share options (Messages, Mail, social apps, etc.)
  • Advertising partners: ad partners such as Google AdMob may process data as described above to serve ads
  • Service providers: trusted providers that help us operate the App (for example, ad networks), subject to their policies and applicable law
  • Legal requirements: if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process

Data Retention

Screen recordings remain on your device until you delete them from the App or from your Photos library. Locally stored App preferences remain until you uninstall the App or clear app data.

Advertising partners may retain data according to their own retention policies.

Children’s Privacy

XRecorder is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access, correct, or delete personal information we process
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Withdraw consent for advertising where consent is the legal basis
  • Manage ad personalization through in-app privacy choices or device settings
  • Limit ad tracking on iOS via Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking
  • Reset advertising ID on Android via Settings → Google → Ads

To exercise your rights, contact us using the email below. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by applicable law.

International Users

If you use the App from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where our service providers operate, including the United States. By using the App, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed through the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. You are responsible for securing your device and choosing who you share recordings with.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
